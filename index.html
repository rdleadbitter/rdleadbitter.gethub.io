Problem statement: Overcrowding on Campus

Many student facilities at USC are becoming overcrowded. This leads to inconsistent food wait times and an inability to complete activities like physical activity and getting academic help.

Affinity diagram: Overcrowding on Campus

We came up with ideas in the categories: user security, functionality, design, advertisement, accuracy, information input. Our ideas are mostly related to the app's front end design and capabilities. We also considered how the app stores and shares the users' data.

Team Personas: Overcrowding on Campus

"Everywhere's always so darn crowded" -Frank Simpleton, 19 y/o Business Major at the University of South Carolina.

Team Storyboards: Overcrowding on Campus

A student goes to Russell House hoping to get lunch between classes, only to find it is far more crowded than the GrubHub app indicated and their order will take too long. This reminds the student to use their campus crowding tracker app, which shows a high level of crowdedness.

Team Sketches: Time Travelers

A user can view crowded levels at various locations and events, accurate estimated wait times for food, and view where their friends are on campus.

Team Paper Prototype: Time Travelers
